## 2. Return Eligibility

### 2.1. Time Frame
You may return most new, unopened items within {{ecommerce:return_period}} of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original packaging and include all accessories, manuals, and documentation.

### 2.2. Return Condition
To be eligible for a return, your item must be:
- In the same condition that you received it
- Unused and in its original packaging
- Complete with all included items
- Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

### 2.3. Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Digital products
- Personal care items (for hygiene reasons)
- Perishable goods
- Custom-made or personalized products
- Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable"
- Items that have been worn, used, or altered

## 3. Return Process

### 3.1. Initiating a Return
To start the return process:
1. Contact our customer service team at {{company:email}} or call {{company:phone}}
2. Provide your order number, the items you wish to return, and the reason for return
3. Our team will provide you with a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
4. Include this RMA number on the return package

### 3.2. Return Shipping
You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless:
- You received an incorrect or defective item
- The item was damaged in transit
- We specifically state that return shipping is covered

We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance for your protection. We cannot be responsible for items lost or damaged in return transit.

## 4. Refunds

### 4.1. Processing Time
Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to determine if it meets our return policy requirements. If approved:
- Refunds will be processed within {{ecommerce:refund_time}} of receiving the returned item
- You will receive an email notification when your refund is processed

### 4.2. Refund Method
Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:
- Credit card charges will be refunded to the same card
- PayPal payments will be refunded to your PayPal account
- Other payment methods will be refunded as appropriate

### 4.3. Refund Amount
Your refund will include:
- The purchase price of the returned item(s)
- Any taxes charged on the returned item(s)

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, defective product, etc.).

## 5. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product:
1. Follow the return process above
2. In your communication with our customer service, specify that you want an exchange and provide details of the replacement item
3. If there is a price difference, it will be charged or refunded accordingly

## 6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the item
2. Provide photos of the damage or defect
3. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at our expense

## 7. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within {{ecommerce:refund_time}} after we've confirmed its processing:
1. Check your bank account or credit card statement again
2. Contact your credit card company or bank, as it may take some time for the refund to officially post
3. Contact us at {{company:email}} if you've done the above and still haven't received your refund

## 8. Sale Items

Items purchased on sale are subject to the same return policy as regularly priced items unless otherwise specified at the time of purchase.

## 9. Cancellations

1. Contact our customer service immediately at {{company:email}} or {{company:phone}}
2. If the order hasn't shipped, we'll process a full refund
3. If the order has already shipped, you'll need to follow our return procedure once you receive the item
