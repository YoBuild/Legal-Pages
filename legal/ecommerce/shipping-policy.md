## 5. Order Tracking

### 5.1. Tracking Information
Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by:
- Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
- Logging into your account on our website
- Contacting our customer service

### 5.2. Delivery Updates
Our shipping carriers provide estimated delivery dates, but these are not guaranteed. Factors such as weather conditions and high volume periods may affect delivery times.

## 6. Shipping Issues

### 6.1. Lost or Delayed Packages
If your package appears to be lost or significantly delayed:
1. Check the tracking information for any updates
2. Contact the shipping carrier directly for more information
3. If the issue persists, please contact our customer service at {{company:email}}

### 6.2. Damaged Packages
If your package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and contents
2. Contact us at {{company:email}} within 48 hours of delivery
3. We will work with you to resolve the issue by sending a replacement or issuing a refund

## 7. Delivery and Acceptance

### 7.1. Delivery Confirmation
We may require a signature for certain high-value items. If no one is available to sign for the package, the carrier may leave a delivery attempt notice with instructions for rescheduling or pickup.

### 7.2. Incorrect Address
You are responsible for providing accurate shipping information. If a package is returned to us due to an incorrect address, the customer will be responsible for any additional shipping charges to redeliver the package.
