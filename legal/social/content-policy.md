## 3. Prohibited Content

The following types of content are prohibited on our platform:

### 3.1. Illegal Content
- Content that violates any applicable local, state, national, or international law
- Content that promotes illegal activities
- Content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others

### 3.2. Harmful Content
- Content that threatens, harasses, bullies, or intimidates others
- Content that promotes self-harm or suicide
- Content that depicts or promotes violence or physical harm
- Hate speech or content that attacks people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or medical condition
- Content that contains graphic or gratuitous violence

### 3.3. Privacy Violations
- Content that violates the privacy of others
- Sharing personal or confidential information about others without their consent
- Impersonating other individuals or organizations

### 3.4. Spam and Deceptive Practices
- Spam, unsolicited commercial messages, or excessive promotional content
- Misleading or deceptive content
- Manipulating platform features (such as likes or follows) to artificially increase visibility
- Creating fake accounts or using bots

### 3.5. Adult Content
- Sexually explicit content
- Content intended to arouse sexual excitement
- Nudity (with limited exceptions for educational, artistic, or medical contexts)

## 6. Special Provisions for Specific Content Types

### 6.1. News Content
Content presented as news or current events must not be deliberately misleading or false. We may add labels or context to content containing unverified claims or misleading information.

### 6.2. Political Content
Political content is permitted, but we may apply labels to political advertisements or content from state-controlled media organizations.

### 6.3. Health Information
Content providing health information should be factually accurate and not make misleading claims. We may add context or labels to health information that contradicts public health experts.
